About Akan Names

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.This naming tradition is shared throughout West Africa and the African diaspora.

Akan Day Names

Monday Kwadwo Adwoa

Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa

Notable figures with day names include Ghana's first president Kwame Nkrumah and former United Nations Secretary-General Kofi Annan.
